Welcome to Pivot Counselling & Training.

At Pivot Counselling & Training, we offer counselling in a safe, respectful, and supportive environment where you can slow down and make sense of what you are experiencing. We understand that reaching out can feel like a significant step, and our aim is to create a space where you feel heard, understood, and supported. Whether you or a loved one are navigating anxiety, low mood, relationship difficulties, grief, or major life transitions, we work collaboratively with you to find a way forward that feels meaningful and supportive.

We are delighted to welcome you and hope this page gives you a sense of who we are, what we value, and how we may be able to support you.

Anxiety, Stress & Trauma

Pivot Counselling is committed to walking with you as you deal with:

  • Ongoing anxiety or worry
  • Panic attacks
  • Social or performance anxiety
  • Trauma and post-traumatic stress
  • Difficulty switching off or relaxing
  • Stress, overwhelm, or burnout

Anxiety, stress, and trauma can affect the way a person thinks, feels, and moves through daily life. At times, the effects can be obvious and intense. At other times, they can sit quietly in the background, shaping how safe, settled, or steady a person feels.

Anxiety can show up in many ways such as, including persistent worry, physical tension, panic, difficulty sleeping, or a sense of being constantly on edge. For some of us, these experiences are also shaped by past or recent trauma, making it harder to feel settled, safe, or able to switch off.

Depression & Low Mood

Your pivot counsellor is professionally focused on helping you face:

  • Persistent low mood or sadness
  • Loss of motivation or energy
  • Feeling disconnected or numb
  • Reduced enjoyment in life
  • Burnout or emotional exhaustion
  • Loss of direction or meaning

We support those experiencing ongoing low mood, loss of motivation, or a sense of disconnection from life. This may include reduced enjoyment, fatigue, or difficulty finding direction and meaning.

We are also committed to supporting individuals navigating burnout, longer-term depression, or the emotional impact of life changes and unmet expectations.

Grief & Loss

Your Pivot counsellor can support you to make sense of your experience and find a way forward, while honouring what has been lost. This may include:

  • Loss of a loved one
  • Relationship separation or divorce
  • Life transitions (e.g. relocation, career change)
  • Feelings of longing, numbness, or disorientation
  • Adjusting to change and redefining identity

Grief can show up in many ways including sadness, numbness, longing, or a sense of disorientation. At Pivot, we support people navigating the impact of loss, whether through the death of a loved one, relationship breakdown, or significant life change.

We recognise that grief is deeply personal and does not follow a fixed timeline.

Marriage & Relationships

Your Pivot counsellor will create a space to talk openly and work carefully with these concerns, supporting greater understanding, steadiness, and connection. This may include:

  • Conflict and communication difficulties
  • Repeated arguments or unhelpful relational patterns
  • Emotional disconnection or loneliness within the relationship
  • Trust issues, betrayal, and repair
  • Relationship stress and strain
  • Separation and relationship transitions
  • Parenting stress impacting the couple relationship
  • Intimacy and sexual concerns
  • Rebuilding closeness, understanding, and connection

Close relationships can be a deep source of love, support, and meaning, but they can also become places of hurt, tension, and disconnection. When communication breaks down, trust is damaged, or conflict becomes repetitive, partners can feel stuck, distant, or unsure how to move forward.

At other times, it can be less obvious but no less painful — a loss of closeness, growing resentment, loneliness, or a sense that things are no longer as they once were. Intimacy concerns may also be present, including difficulties with affection, emotional or sexual closeness, or feeling understood by one another.

Child, Adolescent & Family

Your Pivot counsellor will create a supportive space to understand what is happening for your child or family, and work with you to respond with care, clarity, and practical direction. This may include support with:

  • Anxiety, stress, or emotional overwhelm in children and adolescents
  • Behavioural concerns and emotional regulation difficulties
  • School-related stress, friendship issues, or social challenges
  • Low mood, withdrawal, or loss of confidence
  • Family conflict and communication difficulties
  • Parenting challenges and relational strain at home
  • Separation, blended family, or co-parenting adjustments
  • Life transitions, grief, loss, or significant change
  • Strengthening understanding, connection, and support within the family

Children, adolescents, and families can go through seasons that feel stressful, confusing, or emotionally heavy. A child may struggle to express what is happening, while an adolescent may feel overwhelmed, withdrawn, anxious, or misunderstood. Family life can also come under strain when emotional distress, behavioural concerns, conflict, or major transitions begin to affect the home.

Your Pivot counsellor provides a space to better understand these experiences, respond with care, and find thoughtful, practical ways forward. This may involve supporting a young person individually, working alongside parents, or helping families make sense of patterns contributing to tension, disconnection, or distress.

Training and Professional Development

Pivot counselling provides a wealth of professional support for individuals facing a particular need, trauma or relationship issue. However, the best treatment may well be preparation, training us to be prepared for, aware of and able to grow before these events occur.

Individual and personal Training can play an important role in strengthening insight, building practical skills, and supporting healthier ways of relating, working, and responding to challenge. At Pivot training we offer a growth and resilience training that is thoughtful, engaging, and grounded in both professional experience and real-world application.

Our training can be tailored to a range of settings; workplaces, schools, community organisations, and practitioner groups.

This training may build you and your team in:

  • Mental health and wellbeing
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Communication and relational health
  • Psychosocial education
  • Workplace well-being and team functioning
  • Professional development for therapists and helping professionals
